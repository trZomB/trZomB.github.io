Corporate & Business


City of London Corporation

The City of London Corporation is the municipal governing body of the historic centre of London. During the challenging early days of the COVID-19 pandemic in England, staff training videos were created to help make adjustments and keep everyone safe. Scripts were provided and I recorded the narration for a handful of these projects.


"Nice to work with" - S.Johal (City of London Corporation)


Video Games


Who Wants to be a Mudokon?

In 2013, joint video game developers Oddworld Inhabitants & Just Add Water held public auditions to voice characters in their latest title "Oddworld: New 'n' Tasty". The game is a remake of a childhood favourite of mine; "Oddworld: Abe's Oddysee". My "Mudokon" is a non-playable character and appears multiple times throughout the game.
